Privacy Policy for Bible Study App

Last updated: August 6, 2025

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Bible Study App ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application (the "App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience using our Bible study tools.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Favorites and Bookmarks: Questions and dictionary terms you save as favorites are stored locally on your device
  • Search History: Your search terms are temporarily stored to improve your experience

Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Information: We may collect basic device information for app functionality and crash reporting
  • Usage Analytics: Anonymous usage statistics to help improve the app experience

How We Use Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the app's functionality
  • Save your favorite questions and dictionary entries
  • Improve search results and user experience
  • Fix bugs and improve app performance
  • Provide customer support when needed

Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage: Your favorites, bookmarks, and preferences are stored locally on your device using SQLite database
  • No Cloud Storage: We do not store your personal study data on external servers
  • Security: We implement appropriate technical measures to protect your information

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties, except:

  • Anonymous analytics data with app improvement services
  • When required by law or to protect our rights

Children's Privacy

Our app is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app contains only educational religious content appropriate for all users.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Delete all locally stored data by uninstalling the app
  • Clear your search history and favorites within the app
  • Contact us with privacy-related questions

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes through the app or this page. Your continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
